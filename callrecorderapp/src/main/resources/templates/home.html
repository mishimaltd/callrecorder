Frequently Asked Questions

How does your service work?

We provide a simple automated call recording service that you can use to record any outgoing phone call in high-quality audio. To use our service you just need to:

  1. Register an account online. It's quick and easy.
  2. Call our toll free automated number.
  3. You will be asked to enter the number of the person you want to connect to on your telephone keypad followed by the '#' key.
  4. The service will read back that number to you and ask you to press '1' on your telephone keypad to confirm.
  5. The service will then connect you to that number. The person being called will see your number on the incoming call, not ours.
  6. At the end of your call just hang up and we'll send you an email with a secure link to your recording.
  7. You can access any of your call recordings at any time by going to your private account dashboard.

Can I try it out before registering?

Absolutely! You can try out our service for free by calling our toll-free trial number. At the end of your call we'll send you a text message with a link to your securely-stored recording.

Our trial service is limited to a maximum call duration of five minutes, your call will automatically disconnect after five minutes has elapsed.

How do I get access to my recording?

At the end of your call we'll automatically send you an email with a link to your securely-stored recording.

You can also access any of your saved recordings at any time by going to your secure account dashboard.

How does the billing and payment work?

You'll be charged 15¢ per minute for your call recording. Billing starts from the moment the other party picks up the phone and stops when you end the call.

Payment on your account is settled on a monthly basis based on your usage. You only pay for what you use at 15¢ per minute. If you don't use the service, you don't pay anything.

Our service uses a toll free number so your telephone provider will not charge you at all for using our service.

What happens if nobody picks up?

We only start charging you from the moment your call is connected to your intended recipient at 15¢ per minute. If nobody picks up the call then we won't charge you anything.
